About Satika

Tradition Woven with Elegance

Since 1995, Satika has been synonymous with authentic Indian handloom sarees that celebrate the rich cultural heritage of India. Each saree is a masterpiece, meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have inherited these traditional techniques over generations.

Our collection represents the diverse weaving traditions from across India - from the luxurious Banarasis of Varanasi to the vibrant Kanjeevarams of Tamil Nadu, the delicate Chanderis of Madhya Pradesh to the elegant Tant from West Bengal.
